About our company

Who we are

We plan and prepare. We execute with precision.

We can handle small and large events. We are adept at managing every segment of an event to ensure it runs smoothly and on time. We can handle high-profile conference participants from Prime Ministers and Presidents to EU officials and industry leaders. We can interact with audiences to enhance their experience and to ensure they feel connected to an event.

The need to redress gender imbalances on panels is an essential part of our mission and that’s why we are building an accomplished team of leading female moderators.

Our pool of talented male and female moderators come from broadcasting and journalist backgrounds. They are fluent English speakers, are easy to work with, and are expert at interacting with panellists, participants and audiences.

Our history

Planet Podium was founded in 2012 by the award-winning Irish broadcaster, journalist and author Karen Coleman. A former BBC foreign correspondent, Karen is a very experienced journalist who is highly skilled in moderating high-level European and international conferences. Karen reports on European Affairs from Brussels, Strasbourg and Dublin and she brings her insights and knowledge of the EU to Planet Podium’s dynamic team.

Why choose us

Karen Coleman

Karen is one of Ireland’s most accomplished journalists who presented her own national radio show on Newstalk radio in Ireland for many years. A former BBC war correspondent, Karen has been covering Irish and international affairs for radio and television for over two decades. She has worked for both the public and commercial broadcast industries including a ten-year stint with the BBC.

Award Winning

Karen has won several awards for her radio work including a prestigious Irish award for her radio documentary on the effects of the Iraq war.

Prior to her return to Ireland in 1999, Karen spent 10 years with the BBC working as a reporter, producer and foreign correspondent for BBC radio and television. Karen was the BBC’s Balkans correspondent from 1996-1998. She also worked as a television producer for the United Nations in the Balkans in 1995. Her other international assignments have included reporting from the US, Colombia, Albania, Indonesia, Northern Cyprus, Israel/West Bank/Gaza Strip, Kenya, Burmese refugee camps on the Thai/Burma border, Japan and most EU countries.

Global Stage

Karen’s growing interest in European affairs led her to Brussels and Strasbourg where she now reports on the European Parliament. She writes regular newspaper and magazine columns for publications. Her articles have been published in the Irish Independent, the Irish Times, the Sunday Business Post, the Irish Examiner the Irish Daily Mail. She frequently appears as a commentator on RTE television and TV3.

International

Planet Podium provides professional moderators, facilitators and chairs for conferences, seminars and events in Europe and other international locations. Our team is made up of accomplished television and radio broadcasters who can chair conferences on a range of topics from current affairs and business to EU issues, political, social, human rights, environmental and other subjects.
